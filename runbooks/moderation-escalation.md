# Moderation Escalation

## Signal

- A report requires operator review
- Moderator scope is insufficient for the requested action
- High-risk abuse affects a public room or relay standing

## Immediate Actions

1. Identify the reporting pubkey, the affected content, and the current local standing for each subject.
2. Freeze further privilege changes on the affected scope until the operator reviews the case.
3. Preserve the relevant audit trail and event references.

## Recovery

1. Apply the least invasive local action that stops ongoing harm.
2. Record the action in the audit log with subject, scope, and grantor.
3. Notify moderators of the final decision and any scope changes.

## Follow-Up

- Check whether policy rules need refinement.
- Review whether new room-specific permissions are required.
